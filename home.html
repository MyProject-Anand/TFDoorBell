Touch Free DoorBell using IR (Infrared) sensor




ABSTRACT

In this Covid time Door-bell is a medium of transferring virus as it is used maximum times in our daily routine and hence the chances of spreading virus increases. “keeping in this mind, I have designed this project so that it can become a small part of saving this world. The doorbell, mounted outside the front door of his house, is accompanied by a message that inform visitors: “Let’s break the chain. Show your hand! No need to ring the bell.



EXISTING SYSTEM

In the existing system the original Doorbell is not connected just the buzzer.

In the existing system the extra switch is not available because some people doesn't know how to use the hand free bell.

The drawbacks of the existing system is that the buzzer does not produce that much sound.




PROPOSED SYSTEM

The proposed system consist of original Doorbell with the help of Relay.

The proposed system consist of a extra switch for the people who doesn't know how to use the hand free bell.

The Advantage of the proposed system is that the sound is good enough to hear it from every inch of the house.



WORKING PRINCIPLE

The Micro-controller which we are using is the brain of the entire system. Here we have IR (Infrared) sensor and Relay use to controll te 240V, In which it acts as a switch. The Arduino UNO is a Micro-controller which get the signal from the ultra sonic sensor and the UNO set the Relay to high and the Door bell rings.
